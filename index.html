Medical Aid

Access to healthcare is not a privilege, it is a right. At HFF, we bring medical care to those who would otherwise go without.

Every treatment is more than medical care — it is dignity restored, independence regained, and hope renewed.

Education & Opportunity

Education is the foundation for ending poverty and creating hope. At HFF, we invest in brighter futures by giving children and youth access to quality learning opportunities.

Each book, classroom, and mentor becomes a stepping stone toward a life of dignity and opportunity.

Food Security

HFF fights hunger by providing reliable access to nutritious meals for millions each year. We bring essential nourishment to those who need it most.

Shelter & Seasonal Support

We work to protect vulnerable communities from extreme weather by providing shelter, warm clothing, and essentials year-round.

Water Infrastructure

Every drop of clean water restores health, dignity, and hope. We develop and rehabilitate sustainable water systems that bring long-term relief.

Care for Orphans

We support orphaned children with nutritious food, quality education, healthcare, and secure housing.

Faith-Based Giving

Faith-driven giving fulfills our responsibility toward others. We turn Fidya, Kaffarah, and Fitra into vital, life-saving support.
